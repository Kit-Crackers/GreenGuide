About GreenGuide 🌱

GreenGuide is a smart plant care platform built to help people understand, care for, and grow plants better — without confusion.

Many plants suffer not because of neglect, but because of incorrect care. GreenGuide solves this by providing personalized care tips based on plant type and condition.

Whether you're a beginner or a plant enthusiast, GreenGuide becomes your reliable companion in building a healthier green space.

Why GreenGuide Is Needed 🌍

  • 🌿 Plants are dying unnecessarily
    Most people overwater, underwater, or place plants in wrong lighting.
  • 📚 Information is scattered
    Care tips are inconsistent across the internet and hard to trust.
  • 🧠 No personalization
    Every plant is different — generic advice doesn’t work.
  • 🌱 Sustainable living matters
    Healthy plants improve air quality and mental well-being.
